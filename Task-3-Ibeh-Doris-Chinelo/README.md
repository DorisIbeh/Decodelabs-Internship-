
DecodeLabs E-Commerce Business Analysis

Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing e-commerce transaction data using SQL to uncover valuable business insights. The analysis explores product performance, customer spending behavior, payment methods, promotional effectiveness, customer retention, and referral source performance.

The goal of this project is to support data-driven business decisions through structured SQL queries and analytical reporting.

Objectives

The analysis answers the following business questions:

* Which products generate the highest revenue and sales volume?
* Which products experience the most cancellations or returns?
* Which customers spend more than $1,000 successfully?
* What payment methods are most preferred by customers?
* Which coupon promotions drive the most revenue?
* Are customers returning to make repeat purchases?
* Which referral sources generate the most orders and revenue?

Tools Used
SQL Server( the cleaned task 2 dataset was imported to SQL Server)

Key Insights
* Printers were the best-selling products by revenue and units sold.
* SAVE10 generated the highest promotional revenue.
* Instagram was the highest-performing referral source.
* Customer retention was low, with most customers making only one successful purchase.
* Online payments and gift cards were the most popular payment methods.

Recommendations
* Invest more in high-performing products and marketing channels.
* Improve customer retention through loyalty and engagement programs.
* Reduce product returns through better quality control and customer support.
