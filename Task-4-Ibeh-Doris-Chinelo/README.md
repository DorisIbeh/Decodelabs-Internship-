GOAL : Using the cleaned dataset from task two, create a visual representations of data to communicate insights clearly.

TOOL USED: Microsoft Excel 

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
This analysis assessed sales performance, revenue trends, and unrealized revenue using descriptive statistics, PivotTables, correlation analysis, and interactive dashboards. The results highlight substantial revenue loss driven by cancelled, pending, and returned orders, with unrealized revenue surpassing completed sales. Instagram stood out as the most effective referral channel, while some products generated high gross revenue but also recorded significant unrealized sales.

METHODOLOGY
1. Cleaned and structured the dataset for analysis
2. Utilized PivotTables and Pivot Charts
3. Conducted descriptive statistical analysis
4.  Performed outlier detection and correlation analysis
5. Developed an interactive Excel dashboard with slicers and KPI indicators

KEY INSIGHT

Overall Performance
*High Gross Sales ($1.26M) but low realized sales ($488.8K) → indicates significant revenue leakage.
* Failed Sales ($776K) are higher than successful sales, which is a major concern.
* Total Orders: 1,200 with Average Sale ≈ $1,054.

 Sales Efficiency Issues
* More than 60% of potential revenue is lost due to failed transactions.
* High cancellation and return rates (each around 20%) show operational inefficiencies.

 Product Insights
* Top-performing products: Chair and Printer (~$195K each).
* Lowest performer: Phone (~$151K).
* However, Chair and Laptop also have the highest cancellations, suggesting demand exists but fulfillment issues persist.

Trend Analysis
* Sales fluctuate over time with no consistent growth pattern.
* Recent periods show slight recovery after dips, but still unstable.

 Customer Acquisition
* Instagram ($108K) is the strongest referral source.
* Followed by Email and Google, while Referral traffic is weakest.

 Order Status Breakdown
* Orders are almost evenly split across: 
   * Delivered (19%)  
  * Pending (20%) 
   * Returned (20%)  
  * Cancelled (21%)
* Indicates logistics and fulfillment bottlenecks.

BUSINESS INTERPRETATION
1. High unrealized sales point to inefficiencies in order processing and conversion
2. Instagram and Email channels are more effective in driving revenue
3. Frequent cancellations and returns may reflect issues with customer satisfaction, delivery, or payment systems
4. Revenue leakage limits the organization’s ability to convert potential earnings into actual income.
   
RECOMMENDATIONS
1. Identify and address the root causes of cancellations and returns
2. Strengthen order fulfillment and payment verification processes
3. Accelerate follow-ups on pending orders to improve conversion rates
4. Increase investment in high-performing channels such as Instagram
5. Track and analyze high-value transactions for insights and risk management
6. Enhance customer experience and retention strategies to reduce returns.
